TITLE V
From the Collective Consumption Convention

         Article 107. Civil consumer entities and associations of suppliers or trade unions of an economic category may regulate, by written agreement, consumer relations that are intended to establish conditions relating to price, quality, quantity, warranty and characteristics of Products and services, as well as the complaint and composition of the consumer conflict.

         § 1 The agreement shall become mandatory upon registration of the instrument in the title and document office.

         § 2 The agreement shall only bind the members to the signatory entities.

         § 3 The supplier who disconnects from the entity at a date subsequent to the registration of the instrument is not exempted from complying with the agreement.

         Art. 108. (Vetoed).
